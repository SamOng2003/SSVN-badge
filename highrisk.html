Về Survival Skills Vietnam (SSVN)
Survival Skills Vietnam (SSVN) is Vietnam's leading accredited emergency response training organisation, established in 2014. Through the VitalMark™ Dynamic Safety Ecosystem, SSVN delivers continuous, AI-maintained first aid certification to high-risk industries — replacing one-time training certificates with a living, verifiable safety standard.
"Knowledge Decay erases 70% of first aid skills within 3 months. VitalMark™ exists because one training session is not enough — and in high-risk industries, that gap costs lives."
